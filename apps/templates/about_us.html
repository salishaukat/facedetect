Scholar Jeffrey C. Stewart

Won the Pulitzer Prize 2019 for biography

Jeffrey C. Stewart is a writer, curator, and professor of the history and culture of America, with a focus on race and the aesthetic brilliance of the Black community. Born in Chicago, Stewart attended UCLA, eventually graduating with honors in philosophy from the University of California at Santa Cruz in 1971.
